Reservations

The /Pub is open for drinks on Wednesday and Thursday, but that does not mean that the /Pub is not available the rest of the week. Besides the planned events and the regular opening hours (Wednesday and Thursday from 16:00), it is also possible to reserve the /Pub, for example for your graduation drinks or a lunch. Note that an event with 50 or more visitors, or an event with an end time later than 19:00 should be communicated at least 2 weeks before the event.

Mobitap

The Mobitap is a beautiful piece of state of the art engineering. The mobitap is, what the name suggests it is: A flexible, easily movable and mobile beer tap unit. The Mobitap enables the /Pub, and also especially external parties to pour their precious liquid gold from anywhere they want! It is especially useful for outside activities such as CoBo’s, BBQ’s and Buitenborrels.
